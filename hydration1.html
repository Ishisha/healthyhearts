Hydration

It's a brain power booster. More than half your body is composed of water. Water is critical for vital organs to function.

Types of Hydration

Fruits, Gatorate and Electrolyte filled fluids are healthy

Creative ways to remember to hydrate

Tie a yellow ribbon on your dominant arm. Download the hydration app set and alarm on your phone

Nutrition and hydration go hand in hand

A balance of sodium and potassium is important when trying to stay hydrate

Exercise and hydrtation

Sips of water while you play. At least 60 minutes of play time a day. You can organize a scavenger hunt or invite your friend to go bike riding
